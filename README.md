# 🎮 GameList

**GameList** is a simple web app that helps you manage participants in MOBA-style matches. You can select 2–5 players per match, queue them up, and track wins and losses in a match history.

---

## 🌐 Live Demo

➡️ [https://raclesty.github.io/gamelist](https://raclesty.github.io/gamelist)

---

## 📋 How to Use (English)

1. **Add participants** by typing their name and clicking "Add".
2. From the list, **select 2 to 5 participants** for a match.
3. Click **"Add to Match List"** to queue them up.
4. In the match queue:
   - Click **"Win"** or **"Lose"** to complete the match.
   - Click **"Delete"** to remove a match.
5. Completed matches will appear in the **Match History** below.

---

## 📋 Cómo Usar (Español)

1. **Agrega participantes** escribiendo su nombre y haciendo clic en "Add".
2. De la lista, **selecciona entre 2 y 5 participantes** para una partida.
3. Haz clic en **"Add to Match List"** para añadirlos a la lista de partidas.
4. En la cola de partidas:
   - Haz clic en **"Win"** o **"Lose"** para completar la partida.
   - Haz clic en **"Delete"** para eliminar una partida.
5. Las partidas completadas aparecerán en el **Historial de Partidas**.
